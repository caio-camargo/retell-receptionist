Frequently asked questions

How long does setup actually take? +

Five minutes for the basics: paste your URL, pick a voice, get a number. Most customers spend another 10–15 minutes refining tone and adding calendar access before going live.

How natural does it actually sound? +

Natural enough that callers stay on the line and book. HealthJoy chose Retell specifically on voice quality after testing alternatives. You can pick from thousands of voices across ElevenLabs, Cartesia, and Retell's own — including the ones already running millions of enterprise calls a month.

Do my callers know they're talking to AI? +

Yes — and that's by design. Retell ships with disclosure prompts built in, and a growing number of jurisdictions now require them (California's AB 2905, the EU AI Act, FCC proposed rules). Compliance is on by default, so you don't have to think about it. The voice quality is good enough that disclosure doesn't tank engagement.

What happens when the AI can't handle a call? +

It transfers — warm or cold — to whichever staff member you've configured. You'll also get a transcript and summary in your inbox or CRM.

Is it HIPAA compliant? +

Yes. Retell is HIPAA-eligible and SOC 2 Type II certified, with BAAs available on the Business plan and above. Zero Data Retention is available for regulated workloads.

Can it use my existing phone number? +

Port your number, forward your line, or use a fresh Retell-provisioned number. Whatever's least disruptive to your operation.

What does it cost? +

Per-minute pricing with a free trial. No per-seat fees, no setup fees. See the pricing page for current rates.

What if I need something more custom? +

Drop into the full Retell Agent Platform — same account, same number, same voice. Build custom flows, plug in your own LLM, integrate any system. No migration.

How is this different from ElevenAgents Reception or other AI receptionists? +

Two things: the platform underneath, and the absence of lock-in. Retell Receptionist is built on infrastructure that already runs enterprise call centers — and you can swap the LLM, voice, or telephony provider any time without rebuilding.
